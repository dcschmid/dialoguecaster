#### Technology as a Driver of Style

- Multitrack Recording enabling studio experimentation and layered arrangements
- Drum Machines introducing quantized rhythm and machine aesthetics
- Affordable Synthesizers opening access to electronic sound design and home studios
- Sampling Technology shaping loop culture and intertextual composition
- Auto-Tune shifting from pitch correction to deliberate aesthetic choice
- DAWs and Plugins redefining production through bedroom studios
- Streaming Algorithms influencing song length, pacing and structural hooks
- Short-Form Platforms reshaping composition toward immediacy and repeatability
- Music Television, Visual Identity and Performance redefining artist personas
